Beneficiaries of Zakat

The Holy Quran (9:60) classifies the due recipients of zakat under the following eight categories.

label The poor (Faqir/Fuqara)
label The needy (Miskeen)
label The administrators of zakat (Amil)
label The sympathizers (Muallaf-at-Quloobuhum)
label To free slaves (Riqab)
label Those who are in debt (Gharimin)
label For the cause of God (Fisabillillah)
label Those who are stranded during a journey (Ibnus Sabil)
