Zakat Reminder

Ease your way

Zakat Reminder is a web application that ease the process of Zakat for muslims and provide you the services you need to make it right and maintain the third pillar of Islam

  • Allow the user to obtain a secure space: create an account with their email address and password, then activate their account via email.
  • Allow the user to link the application to their bank account.
  • Help the user to know if he is responsible for the Zakat or not at all times.
  • If he is liable: calculate the amount due.
  • Maintain a list of Zakat beneﬁciaries.
  • If he pays: help the user to keep tracking their payment history.
  • Calculate the remainder of the Zakat at any time.
